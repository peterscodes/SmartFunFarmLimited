about us

Smart Fun Limited has an existing agricultural projects with investment in poultry farm with 4500 layers and 3000 broilers, 6 acres of plantain farm and now proposing to establish 20 hectares of pineapple farm in Abeokuta, Ogun state. The aforementioned officials of the company forms the board of directors. This board will be the highest decision making organ of the company. Furthermore, there is a proposed project implementation committee which include representatives of the following organizations; Smartfun Limited as the investor. First Massive Achievers Limited as the offtaker and technical partner. Agrotrends Nigeria Integrated Services as consulting and management partner. Each partner in this joint venture will play strategic roles based on their areas of strength and experience in ensuring that the farm runs successfully and profitably. In Nigeria, Pineapple is an FMCG fruit with high returns on investment and grows mainly in “Evergreen Savanna”, but most local farmers within the region does not have the financial capacity to cultivate pineapple in large quantity due to its high capital required. There is a very high local market demand for pineapple, most especially the “Smooth Cayenne” while major fruit juice and wine companies are also on the search for reliable and consistent supplier of the produce. Internationally, pineapple is a product that is legally allowed to be traded at the international commodity market. Hence, this brings about another reliable market source for the produce. Our Poultry farm of 4500 layers and 3000 boilers is also among the Nation highly agricultural establishments.
